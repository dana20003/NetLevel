Welcome to NextLevel

The ultimate time management solution. Prioritize your most important tasks, earn rewards with each completion, and level up every three rewards to celebrate your progress. Transform your daily routine into a journey of success with NextLevel!

Features of NextLevel

  • Personalized Study Plans: AI generates study plans based on your preferences.
  • Task Management: Easily add, edit, and manage your tasks.
  • Gamification & Rewards: Earn rewards like gaming or movie time for completing tasks.
  • AI-Powered Scheduling: Let the AI optimize your study and break times.
  • Leveling Up & Achievements: Level up and earn badges as you complete tasks.
  • Customizable Rewards: Choose your rewards based on your preferences.
  • Time Tracking: Track time spent on each task for better focus.
  • Progress Tracker: Visual progress indicators to keep you motivated.
  • Break Day Incentive: Earn a break day for completing all weekly tasks.
  • Task Difficulty & Customization: Set task difficulty levels for better task management.
  • Notifications & Reminders: Receive reminders to stay on track with your tasks.
  • Sync Across Devices: Access your study planner on any device.
  • User Profile & Customization: Personalize your dashboard and update your profile.
  • Help & Support: Access the help section or FAQ for assistance.
